Baara+ Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Baara+! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application for Android and iOS.

Information We Collect

When you use Baara+, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when creating an account.
  • Usage Information: We collect data on how you interact with the app, including pages visited, features used, and other behavioral data.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access Baara+, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Information: With your consent, we may collect your precise location data to provide location-based services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our services
  • Personalizing your experience on Baara+
  • Communicating with you, including responding to inquiries and providing updates
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Ensuring the security of our platform

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

Account Security

Your account security is important to us. Please keep your account credentials confidential and notify us immediately of any unauthorized access or suspicious activity.

Guest Login

While we offer a guest login option to experience the app, please note that certain pages and resources may require you to create an account for access.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. You will be notified of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
